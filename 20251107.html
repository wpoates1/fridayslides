The ROI Reality

AI investments are paying off faster than anticipated. 60% of EMEA leaders expect to achieve their ROI goals within a single year, and confidence in future AI agents is nearly universal.

Top Investment Priorities

When prioritizing AI investments for productivity, leaders are focused on core operations. Increasing efficiency is the clear top priority, followed by enhancing data-driven decision-making and modernizing IT infrastructure.

Tactical activities like content creation (34%) and task automation (36%) are seen as lower priorities compared to these foundational improvements.

Unlocking the Full Potential: 5 Key Priorities

To accelerate ROI and move from tactical gains to strategic transformation, enterprise leaders should focus on five key priorities.

  1. 1

    Establish an Effective Operating Model

    Define a common approach (e.g., federated or hub-and-spoke) with clear ownership to deliver on investment.

  2. 2

    Cultivate AI Literacy

    Build knowledge from the board to entry-level to ensure AI is applied to the right areas for maximum value.

  3. 3

    Get Comfortable with Uncertainty

    Develop a culture that embraces rapid change and enables purposeful, agile innovation in an "AI everywhere" world.

  4. 4

    Understand & Manage Deployment Risks

    Apply AI governance tooling to monitor and mitigate regulatory, reputational, and operational risks like data sharing and bias.

  5. 5

    Establish a Cross-Company "AI Board"

    Define ethical principles and risk appetite, reviewing high-risk use cases to give business units autonomy to implement AI with confidence.
